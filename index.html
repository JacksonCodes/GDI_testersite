How To Find A Bus

Hello! This is a blog about building and living in a bus. Why live in a bus you ask? Well do you enjoy travel? Do you enjoy experencing new places and people? Do you like to wake up to a new sunrise scene every month, or even everyday? Well I sure as heck do. So I am building a bus, to live in.

How To Plan Your Bus

Start with how many people you will have living in your bus.

  • How many people?
  • Do you want a kitchen?
  • Do you want a bathroom?
  • Does your bathroom have a shower?
  • Do you want the bedroom to be seperate from the living space?
  1. I have two people, a couple.
  2. I do want a kitchen.
  3. I do want a bathroom and a shower.
  4. No, I dont care if the bedroom is seperate.

How To Get Insurance For Your Bus

Getting insurance can be tricky. You'll want to get insurance before you build out your bus. This is for two reasons:

  • Your bus will weigh too much to pass inspection.
  • You have to have insurance to get permits.
